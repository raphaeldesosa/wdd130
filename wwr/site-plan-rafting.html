Overview

Purpose

We are White Water Rafting River Adventure. We enjoy water rafting and are very passionate about the joy and fun this activity can bring to oneself or to their family and friends. As seen in our name, this is a river adventure. The advenure consist of a 1 hour run which starts from the Mountains of Davao going down. You will be experiencing a lot of adrenaline fun, a number of different rapids as you go down the mountain. And we want you to experience this! Come and let's have fun in White Water River Adventure located at Davao, Philippines.

Audience

White Water Rafting River Adventure is a place where family and friends can go and have fun and experience the thrill of water rafting.
